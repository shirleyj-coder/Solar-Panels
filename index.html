The Lytegen PPA Program

Our most popular option — the Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) — lets homeowners go solar with no upfront cost. We cover installation, equipment, and maintenance completely free.

Instead of paying for panels, you simply pay for the power they produce — at a lower rate than your utility company. You save money from day one, enjoy clean renewable energy, and never worry about maintenance or repair costs.

Prefer to own your system? We also offer easy financing options and cash purchase discounts for maximum long-term savings.
